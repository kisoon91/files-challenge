Bob Kane was an American comic book artist who, with Bill Finger, created the superhero Batman. In early 1939, Detective Comics' success with the superhero Superman, Kane created Bat-Man by getting idea from the characters of Zorro, ornithopter, and swashbuckler. Bill Finger was an American comic strip and writer. He is the co-creator of the character Batman. He was inducted into the comic book industry's Jack Kirby Hall of Fame and the Will Eisner Comic Book Hall of Fame.

Batman

Batman is a fictionalf superhero from the comic books, which also is referred to "the Dark Knight". Batman's secret identity is Bruce Wayne, who is an American billionaire, the business magnate. When he was a child, he witnesses the murder of his parents, which lead him to revenge on criminals. He then wears a bat-themed costume to fight against crimes. He often fights villains, which includes the Joker, Penguin, Riddler. Althought Batman is superhero, he does not possess any superpowers. So he uses his intelect, martial arts skills, physical prowess, detective skills, science, and wealth on crime.
